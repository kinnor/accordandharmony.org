About Our Foundation

Building a Better Tomorrow Through Unity and Compassion

Our Story

Who We Are

At Accord and Harmony Foundation, we believe in the power of unity and the transformative impact it can have on the lives of those in need.

Founded with a vision to create positive change in our communities, we are dedicated to supporting vulnerable populations and championing educational opportunities for children across Bulgaria. Our work is driven by compassion, transparency, and a commitment to making a lasting difference.

Based in Sofia, Bulgaria, our foundation serves as a beacon of hope for those facing hardship. We understand that true change comes from addressing both immediate needs and long-term development, which is why our programs focus on comprehensive support systems that empower individuals and families to build better futures.

Through strategic partnerships with local organizations, schools, and community leaders, we create sustainable solutions that address the root causes of poverty and educational inequality. Our approach is holistic, recognizing that education, healthcare, and social support are interconnected elements essential for community well-being.

Our Mission

To unite communities in support of those in need and provide educational opportunities that transform lives and build brighter futures for children and families across Bulgaria.

Our Vision

A world where every child has access to quality education and every family has the support they need to thrive, creating harmonious communities built on compassion and mutual respect.

Our Values

We are guided by integrity, compassion, transparency, and unity. We believe in the power of collective action and the importance of treating everyone with dignity and respect.

Our Work

What We Do

Educational Programs

We provide scholarships, school supplies, tutoring services, and educational workshops to ensure every child has the opportunity to learn and grow.

Family Support

Providing essential resources, food assistance, and counseling services to families facing economic hardship and social challenges.

Community Outreach

Organizing community events, workshops, and support groups that bring people together and strengthen social bonds.
