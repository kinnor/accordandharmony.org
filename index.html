Nurturing Young Hearts and Minds

At Accord and Harmony Foundation, we believe every child deserves love, education, and the opportunity to dream big.

Since our founding, we've dedicated ourselves to supporting vulnerable children and families across Sofia, Bulgaria. Through educational programs, essential resources, and mentorship, we help children overcome obstacles and discover their full potential.

From providing school supplies and tutoring to offering meals and safe spaces for learning, we work hand-in-hand with families, schools, and compassionate supporters like you to ensure no child is left behind.

Together, we're creating a world where every child can learn, grow, and thrive‚Äîregardless of their circumstances.
