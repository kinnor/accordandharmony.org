Building a Harmonious Future

At Accord and Harmony Foundation, we stand as a beacon of hope for communities across Sofia, Bulgaria.

Our mission is rooted in the belief that when people come together in harmony, remarkable transformations become possible. We're dedicated to uplifting vulnerable families, empowering children through education, and creating pathways to brighter futures.

Through strategic partnerships with local communities, schools, and compassionate supporters like you, we deliver essential resources, educational opportunities, and comprehensive support services that make a real difference in people's lives.

Join us in building a future where every child has access to quality education and every family finds the support they need to thrive.
